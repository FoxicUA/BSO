Steps of going through
a rental dispute in Dubai

Going through a rental dispute is not rocket science. Still, you need to spend a considerable amount of your time on this procedure. Steps of going through a rental dispute in Dubai are provided below:

01

Prepare all supporting documents, including e-mails between you and your tenant (don’t miss the importance of having clear correspondence between you and your tenant via verified e﻿-﻿mail addresses or registered mobile numbers to involve WhatsApp or SMS messages);

02

Prepare your statement of claims in a legally correct format made in the Arabic language (make sure it’s drafted in a right way with the correct Arabic translation, so the whole claim won’t turn to be against yourself);

03

Issue a legal notice to your tenant (while notifying your tenant don’t forget to register it in UAE public records);

04

File the case in Rental Dispute Center (Rental Dispute Center Dubai contact number is 8004488, you can call here for customer support);

05

Pay the court fees, which are 3.5 % of either your annual rent or claiming amount (minimum AED 350.00 and maximum AED 20,000.00);

06

Have a hearing scheduled and appear on that day;

07

Get the judgment in order to start the execution (the most important stage is the execution process, where you need to know what to do to speed up the matter and avoid long waiting until the result comes).
